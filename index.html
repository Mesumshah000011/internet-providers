Types of Internet Connections in 2025

Before exploring the best internet plans, it's good to know the types of internet connections available this year.

1. Fiber-Optic Internet

Fiber offers super-fast speeds and rock-solid reliability. It's perfect for heavy internet users who stream 4K videos, play online games, or attend virtual meetings.

2. Cable Internet

Cable internet uses the same lines as TV service. It's widely available and usually cheaper than fiber, with high speeds for most households.

3. DSL Internet

DSL runs through telephone lines. It's slower compared to fiber and cable but still a good option in rural places.

4. Fixed Wireless

This type of internet uses radio signals instead of cables. It works well in areas where wired internet is not an option.

5. Satellite Internet

Satellite internet is available almost anywhere. It's often used in rural or remote regions but can have data limits and higher delays.
